Future Plans & Conclusion

For now, the project has been archived; as time goes on, it's going to eventually get deleted and no longer available.
I DO NOT have any plans on continuing or saving the project at this position.
However, I may have some good news; in the near future, I may build a new similar periodic table CLI program.
Instead of hand-coding every aspect of the program, it will be API-based.
I'm kind of sad that I have to give up some amazing features for the program due to this change, though.
I haven't found any API that satisfies me yet. If you manage to find a data-rich periodic table database, please feel free to contact me and let me know!
